The Name of the artist: Ajith Kumar

Ajith Kumar is a prominent Tamil actor known for his versatile acting skills and significant contributions to the Tamil film industry. Born on May 1, 1971, in Hyderabad, India, Ajith began his career in the early 1990s. He gained widespread recognition with films like "Aasai" (1995) and "Kadhal Kottai" (1996). Over the years, he has delivered numerous successful films, including "Mankatha" (2011), "Veeram" (2014), and "Viswasam" (2019). Ajith is affectionately called "Thala" by his fans, which means "leader" in Tamil. He is known for his dedication to his craft, often performing his own stunts, and has a reputation for his humility and down-to-earth personality. Besides acting, Ajith is an avid motorsport enthusiast, participating in professional car racing and other motorsport events. With a career spanning over three decades, Ajith Kumar remains one of the most influential and beloved actors in Tamil cinema.
